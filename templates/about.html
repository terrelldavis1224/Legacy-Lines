Inspiration


Legacy Lines was inspired by the idea of creating a game that captures the essence and appeal of popular titles like Wordle, Pokedoku, and Globle. With the spin of a sports twist, I wanted to create a game that would be fun, engaging like picking over/unders.


Note

If the main player's statistics are tied with another player's statistics, then choosing the 'Under' will yield the correct answer.If Thanasis Antetokounmpo has 0 points and Kevin Durant also has 0 points, then the choice that will yield the correct result is 'Under'.
